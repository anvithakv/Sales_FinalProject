# 🛒 Flipmart Sales Dashboard

## 📌 Overview
The **Flipmart Sales Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze and visualize sales performance, profitability, customer transactions, shipping trends, and geographical sales distribution for the Flipmart retail business.

The dashboard provides business insights through dynamic visualizations and KPI indicators, enabling data-driven decision-making.

---

# 📊 Dashboard Preview

## Main Components
- KPI Summary Cards
- Sales by Shipping Mode
- Monthly Sales Trend by Segment
- Sales by Country Map
- Profit by Category
- Top 5 Sales Sub-Categories
- Interactive Filters & Slicers

# 🚀 Features

## 1️⃣ KPI Summary Cards
Displays overall business performance metrics:
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Quantity Sold
- Total Transactions

---

## 2️⃣ Sales by Shipping Mode
Analyzes sales distribution across shipping methods:
- Standard Class
- Second Class
- First Class
- Same Day

### 📌 Insight
- Standard Class generates the highest sales.
- Same Day shipping contributes the least.

---

## 3️⃣ Monthly Sales Trend by Segment
Shows month-wise sales trends for customer segments:
- Consumer
- Corporate
- Home Office

### 📌 Insight
- Consumer segment contributes the highest revenue.
- Sales increase significantly toward year-end.

---

## 4️⃣ Top 5 Sales – Sub Category
Highlights the best-performing product sub-categories:
- Phones
- Chairs
- Copiers
- Bookcases
- Storage

---

## 5️⃣ Sales by Country
Interactive world map visualization showing geographical sales distribution.

### 📌 Major Sales Regions
- North America
- Europe
- Asia

---

## 6️⃣ Profit by Category
Pie chart representing profit contribution by category:
- Technology
- Office Supplies
- Furniture

### 📌 Insight
- Technology category contributes the highest profit.
